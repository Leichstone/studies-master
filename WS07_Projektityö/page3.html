Introduction

Warframe's mod system allows deep customization, with multiplicative and additive effects that create complex builds. Unlike Darktide or Vermintide, where progression is more linear, here every choice scales the stats in non-trivial, gamebreaking ways.

Warframe builds

Below are some of my favorite Warframe and weapon builds, showcasing both a loadout for Mesa Prime, and Rhino Prime.

Mesa Prime Build

Has a ton of survivability with Shatter Shield and Shooting Gallery , while outputting massive damage with Peacemakers. Nourish helps with energy sustain.

Arcanes: Velocity / Avenger | Max rank
Rhino Prime Build

Maximizes survivability with Iron Skin and buffs damage with Roar - which has exponential scaling with some other damage increases. Excellent for high-level survival missions, as Rhino's tankiness scales with damage received.

Arcanes: Molt Augmented / Truculence | Max rank
Phage Electric/Viral Build

A status-focused build for the Phage, utilizing Electric and Viral damage types to maximize damage over time.

Arcanes: Primary Crux | Max rank
Kuva Sobek Build

Kuva Sobek is easy to wield and Acid Shells have an insanely high scaling with Roar, enabling "room nuking" without much effort.

Arcanes: Primary Crux | Max rank
Regulators Prime Build

Mesa's signature pistols, the Regulators, built for critical hits and high damage output via heat procs

Arcanes: Cascadia Flare
Tenet Plinx Build

A secondary weapon capable of hitting the game's damage cap thanks to multiple counts of exponential scaling.

Arcanes: Secondary Deadhead or Secondary Surge | Max rank
Wyrm Prime Build

Buffs the Warframe with a higher critical damage multiplier and firerate, while also protecting them from the occasional status effect.

Arcanes: N/A
Robot Dog Companion Build

One of the strongest companion builds focusing synergy with spread dozens of statuses, capable of even killing Steel Path enemies with ease.

Arcanes: N/A

Unlike Vermintide or Darktide, where stats tend to scale linearly, Warframe uses multiplicative formulas and have a lot more complex interactions between mods. This means that stacking mods cleverly can yield exponential improvements. Understanding these interactions is crucial to creating optimized builds which work past level 500.
