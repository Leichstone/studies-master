High Protein Cucumber Salad

  • Cucumber
  • Half of a red onion
  • Garlic
  • 160g Greek yogurt
  • Squeeze half of a lemon or use lemon juice
  • ¬Ω tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 150g of cooked chicken breast

  1. Slice the cucumber thinly (use a peeler or mandoline if you have one)
  2. Thinly dice half a red onion, and mince one clove of garlic
  3. Add all of the ingredients into a bowl and shake
  4. Enjoy your high-protein meal!

KFC Supercharger Chicken Wrap

Ingredients for 2 wraps
  • 350g chicken breast
  • 70g plain flour
  • 30g cornflour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • Lettuce
Spice mix
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • Half tsp dried oregano
  • Half tsp dried basil
  • Half tsp dried thyme
  • Half tsp mustard powder
  • Half tsp white pepper
  • Half tsp black pepper
  • Half tsp salt
  • Quarter tsp ground ginger
Supercharger sauce
  • 40g low fat mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • Half tsp smoked paprika
  • Half tsp garlic powder
  • Half tsp onion powder
  • Quarter tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • Half tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper

  1. Mix the flour, cornflour and spices in a bowl
  2. Crack the egg into another bowl and beat it
  3. Slice the chicken into strips
  4. Coat in the flour then egg then flour again
  5. Air fry at 180 degrees for 15 minutes and flip halfway
  6. Mix the sauce ingredients and put in the fridge
  7. Chop the lettuce
  8. Build the wrap with sauce, lettuce, chicken and more sauce
  9. Wrap it up and enjoy!

Chocolate Brownie Cake

Brownie Cake
  • 480 grams Sugar
  • 165 grams AP Flour
  • 50 grams Cocoa Powder
  • 70 grams powdered sugar
  • 1 gram kosher salt
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 220 Grams Kerrygold or 82% butterfat Unsalted butter
  • 180 grams Semi-sweet Chocolate
  • 1 tbsp vanilla paste
  • 15 grams room temperature water
Topping
  • 50-100 grams chocolate chips depending on how nasty you are
  • Maldon Salt

  1. Melt chocolate & butter in a double boiler
  2. Whisk eggs in a bowl, slowly temper in the chocolate-butter mixture
  3. Add a little bit of water and vanilla paste
  4. Combine dry ingredients together by sifting them into a bowl, mix until fully combined
  5. Pour in the chocolate-butter-egg mixture to the dry ingredients bowl, and fold it in until no clumps
  6. Cut out a piece of parchement paper and butter the pan, pour in the batter, and add toppings
  7. Bake at 165 degrees Celsius for 45 minutes, let cool and enjoy!
